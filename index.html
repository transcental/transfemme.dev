how do i get a subdomain?

oh, it's easy! just go to transcental/dns and make a PR updating transfemme.dev.yaml with your DNS records! make sure it passes the tests and i'll merge it in.

how do i get an email?

you can't get an @transfemme.dev email yet, but you're welcome to add DNS records for *@you.transfemme.dev emails. i'd love to add a service to give out @transfemme.dev accounts, but i have no idea how i would do this, if you have ideas, please send me an email (amber@transfemme.dev) or make an issue on my dns repo
